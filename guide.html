Protagonists


Cui Buqu (崔不去)

崔 (Cui) – A common surname
不去 (Buqu) – Means ‘refuse to go’

Commander of the Zuoyue Bureau with two Vice-Commanders under his banner. His subordinates are Qiao Xian and Zhangsun Bodhi who are mostly by his side.


Feng Xiao (凤霄)

凤 (Feng) – Of or related to the Pheonix
霄 (Xiao) – The sky, heaven, clouds

Second Commander of the House of Blades.  His subordinate, Pei Jingzhe, often travels with him.


Other Characters


Pei Jingzhe (裴惊蛰)

A member of the House of Blades who follows Feng Xiao closes. He is a relatively young man with a kind-heart. It is noted by Feng Xiao that Pei Jingzhe is too innocent and naive to know the cruel ways of the world, but Feng Xiao seems to hold some sort of hope in Pei Jingzhe, deliberately pushing his potential from time to time, hoping he could gain enough encouragement to excel as a member of the House of Blades.


Qiao Xian (乔仙)

乔 (Qiao) – Tall or mighty
仙 (Xian) – Fairy or goddess

She is a subordinate of Cui Buqu who sees him as a father/guardian/brother, loves and respects him. Qiao Xian learned the medicial arts, martial arts, so that she could protect Cui Buqu and nurse his health whenever he travels away from the capital. Everyone in the Zuoyue Bureau sees her as his bodyguard or some sort. She seems be very familiar with Cui Buqu's personality.


Zhangsun Bodhi (长孙菩提)

One of the vice commanders of the Zuoyue Bureau, and someone that Cui Buqu puts absolute faith in. Zhangsun is much more stable and calm compared to Qiao Xian, who possesses a hotter temper. His martial arts is excellent. Zhangsun hates schemes and strategies, preferring to do more honest and straightforward work such as holding a blade and killing enemies on the battlefield.


Bing Xuan (冰玄)

A disciple of the Hehuan Sect (yes, the same one from Qianqiu). She is well-received by her sect and her people. It is believed that she is most likely the next Sect Leader of the Hehuan Sect. Another theory that Feng Xiao knew her teacher, Bai Rong, since Feng Xiao was familiar to both the name Hehuan Sect and he was also the martial brother of former sect leader of Faxing Sect, Guang Lingsan.



Yuwen Imperial Family (宇文王族)

Former imperial family. Yuwen Yun was the former Emperor of the Sui Empire shortly before his own Prime Minister and father-in-law, Yang Jian, overthrew him. Yuwen Yun was killed, his Empress, Yang Lihua, was taken back by her father and recrowned Princess Leping.

Yuwen Yun (宇文赟)

Former Emperor before he was overthrown. Said to be a tyrant.


Yang Lihua/Princess Leping (杨丽花/乐平公主)

Daughter of Yang Jian. She was married to Yuwen Yun and had one daughter, Yuwen Eying. She is also the eldest daughter of Emperor Yang Jian and Empress Dugu.


Princess Qianjin (千金公主)

Daughter of Yuwen Yun and unknown mother. She was married to Khan Ishbara and is currently his queen. After her father was overthrown and murdered by Yang Jian, she held immense hatred for him. It was said that the war between Turk and the Sui Empire was because she has convinced her husband to go to war with her father's murderer.

Yang Imperial Family (杨王族)

Current imperial family.

Yang Jian (杨坚)

Formerly the Prime Minister of the country, Yang Jian gathered a rebellion and overthrew his own son-in-law, Yuwen Yun, for he was a tyranny king. He spared his own daughter and granddaughter and remade them princess and members of the royal family. His wife is Empress Dugu, and he has no concubines.


Empress Dugu (独孤皇后))

The only wife of Emperor Yang Jian. An intelligent, sharp-witted and passionate woman. She not only helped her husband in his family's matters, but also participated in the imperial court, which former Empresses were not known for doing.


Yang Lihua/Princess Leping (杨丽花/乐平公主)

See Yuwen Imperial family.


Princess Lanling (兰陵公主)

Fifth daughter of Emperor Yang Jian and Empress Dugu. When she was asked by the Emperor and Empress if she wanted to marry an outstanding general, she begged for a bethrotal to Feng Xiao, shocking her parents. Because of this, Feng Xiao asked be the ambassador who will receive the Khotan Ambassaddor so he could quickly leave the capital.


Yuwen Eying (宇文俄英)

Daughter of Yuwen Yun and Princess Leping. She is 14 years old during her first appearance, an intelligent and matured child although she is still very young. Since she is the only daughter of Princess Leping, she is loved dearly by her mother.



Zuoyue Bureau (左月局)

Established by Empress Dugu, the current Empress of the Sui Empire, wife to Emperor Yang Jian. She established the Zuoyue Bureau as an agency to attend to the matters of the pugilist world, as to not stand in the way of the House of Blades who dealth with matters concerning the capital and within the country. It has one Commander and two Vice-Commanders.
