News and Updates


This site is a fan translation of Wushuang / Unparralleled / Peerless written by Meng Xi Shi. I am just translating Wushuang as a casual hobby, and because I've found no English translation elsewhere and really wanted to get people into it. My biography is over here Joan. Any important or massive change to the site or translations will go here.
